Youngjin Hong

I am a PhD student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Minnesota (UMN), advised by Prof. Changhyun Choi. My research focuses on learning-based robot policies for robust and general robot tasks. I am especially interested in Vision-Language-Action models or applying Vision-Language Models for task planning.

Before joining UMN, I was a research engineer at Hanwha Aerospace, where I developed business proposals for next-generation UGV technologies as well as UGV simulation. I completed my master's and bachelor's in Mechanical Engineering at Sungkyunkwan University (SKKU), advised by Hyungpil Moon.
